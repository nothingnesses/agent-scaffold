### `human-review-queue`: Single living human-decision queue

Decided (`Q-10`): approach A (reuse the Open Questions section) upgraded with approach B's discipline, and made push-plus-pull. Make the plan template's "Open Questions, Decisions, Issues and Blockers" section the single living human-decision queue: each item has a stable id, a one-line ask, a status (`open` / `decided -> folded into <slug>` / `superseded`), and a context pointer. Resolved items are marked resolved, not deleted, so a decision is not addressed twice. The orchestrator updates the queue at every checkpoint as a required step and pushes the open items to the human at each checkpoint rather than relying on the human to pull them (a new human would not know to check, and a pull-only model is fragile).
