### `instrument-magic-filename`: Document the `instrument.md` magic pack filename (SE-7)

Deferred cleanup from the `Q-44` audit (`architecture-audit.explorations/audit-sharp-edges.md`, SE-7). A pack has three content categories, but only two are documented: manifest `[[asset]]` entries and the special-cased `principles.toml`. The third, `instrument.md` (read directly at `src/main.rs:242-244` via `source.read("instrument.md").unwrap_or_default()` for the `{{instrument}}` slot), is an undocumented magic filename; the `unwrap_or_default()` makes an absent or mistyped `instrument.md` silently produce an empty slot rather than an error, so a pack author who typos the path gets no signal. Document the special filenames in the pack-format reference, and optionally warn when the file is absent while `--instrument` is set. No open decision, a known change. Deferred as an independent pack-format cleanup.
