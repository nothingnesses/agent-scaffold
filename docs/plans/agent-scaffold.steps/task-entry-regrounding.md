### `task-entry-regrounding`: stakes-scaled task-entry re-grounding and per-step provenance links (`Q-53`)

Not started. Decided (Socratic, `Q-53`, human, 2026-07-19). Add a workflow step that is a LIGHT, stakes-scaled TASK-ENTRY RE-GROUNDING and human alignment check, run before starting a step and on resume. It is sourced from the DURABLE artifacts (plan Step Detail by slug, ledger RESUME STATE, decision receipts by q_id, findings by path, code by file:line/commit), NOT conversation history: history is ephemeral, harness- and machine-specific, and not VCS-shareable, so a fact found only in history is a durability bug to flush into the artifacts, not to cite; out-of-VCS history pointers were rejected as moving the fragility rather than fixing it. The orchestrator writes a short brief (what the task is, why it exists, the cited evidence including the decision RECEIPT with the human's recorded choice, and what it is about to do) and pushes it for a go/no-go via the human-input contract, scaled to stakes. The step also ADOPTS structured PER-STEP PROVENANCE links (a step -> the decisions, findings, and commits that justify it, mirroring questions' `folded_into`/`receipt`), which `render` can present as "why this step exists". Artifact fidelity is ensured by: capture-at-decision with the human's sign-off (the receipt, with `chosen` validated as a member of `options`, is signed evidence, not a paraphrase); two human-confirmation checkpoints (the fold-in queue-push and this task-entry re-grounding); independent review of a capture against its receipt and the step it changes; structured-over-prose for load-bearing facts (fields cannot paraphrase-drift); and the W4 decided-needs-receipt enforcement (extendable, for example to flag a decided item whose recorded choice is not among its receipt options). Chosen over no-regrounding and over out-of-VCS history pointers. It complements the workflow-driver (`Q-51`), which the driver's design pass found should complement rather than subsume this step. Scope goes through the normal implementer plus review loop; sequenced after Inc 6 of `structured-skeleton` (done).
