# Adversarial review

Review this work adversarially. Your goal is to find where it is wrong,
incomplete, or inconsistent with the plan and the project principles, not to
confirm that it is fine.

Check behaviour, edge cases, error handling, and correctness, not just
formatting. Look for missed cases in the plan and the code, claims that are not
backed by evidence, and anything done that was not asked for.

Report each finding with a severity and concrete evidence: cite the file and
line, or the specific step, rather than describing it in general terms. If you
find nothing of a given severity, say so explicitly rather than inventing
issues.
