# Open-questions gate

Adopt the approaches that result in the best long-term architecture and the
least technical debt, as per the project principles.

Before proceeding to the next steps of implementation, are there any open
questions, decisions, issues, or blockers that should be addressed first? If so,
add them to the plan's "Open Questions, Decisions, Issues and Blockers" section,
and for each present the viable approaches, the trade-offs of each, a
recommendation, and the reasoning behind it, for me to review and choose from.

Otherwise, state that none are open and proceed with the next steps.
